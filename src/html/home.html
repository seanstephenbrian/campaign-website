John Martin for District 65 Board of Education

Putting students first. Rebuilding trust. Recommitting to public education.

John Martin is a father, community member, and leader with a deep-rooted belief in education.

As a member of the District 65 Board of Education, John will put students first by reinstating academics as the top priority for D65 and will work closely with teachers, administration, and parents to build a renewed culture of trust, commitment, transparency, and accountability.
